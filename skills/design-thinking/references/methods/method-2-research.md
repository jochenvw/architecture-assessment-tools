# Method 2: Design Research

## What It Is

Systematic discovery of end user needs through direct engagement — interviews, observations, and surveys.

## Purpose

Bridge the gap between stakeholder assumptions and actual user experiences. Without genuine research, teams build solutions that stakeholders want but users don't need.

## Actions

- Generate context-specific interview questions (rapport, problem discovery, constraints)
- Conduct interviews with AI coaching and real-time follow-up suggestions
- Document environmental constraints and user workarounds — workarounds reveal unmet needs
- Map user workflows and validate assumptions across multiple users
- Combine observation with interviews; constraints shape behavior in ways users don't explicitly recognize

## Success Criteria

- User workflows mapped with environmental constraints documented
- Assumptions validated across multiple users
- User workarounds identified (workarounds = unmet needs)
- Patterns distinguished from individual preferences

## Discovery Framework

- **Curiosity-driven**: "Walk me through a typical day when you need to [core task]", "How do you work around that limitation?", "What info do you wish you had?"
- **Environmental constraints**: "Show me where you do this work", "What factors make this harder?", "How do safety/compliance requirements affect your process?"
- **Capability vs system gaps**: "What skills can't you fully use?", "What takes longer to access than to actually do?"

## Key Insight Pattern

Users typically possess core capabilities but face environmental or workflow constraints preventing effective task completion. Design for constraint removal, not capability building.

## Manufacturing Example

**Discovery**: Factory workers revealed:

- They CAN repair equipment (skill exists)
- Finding manual info takes 10–15 min; actual repair takes 5–10 min
- Environment: 85–90dB noise, greasy hands prevent touchscreens
- Maintenance response time: 60–90 minutes

**Result**: Information access (not repair capability) is the primary constraint.
